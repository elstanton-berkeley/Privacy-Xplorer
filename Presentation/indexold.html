Our Data and Approach

Base Data for training and testing

  1. OPP115 - The OPP-115 Corpus (Online Privacy Policies, set of 115) is a collection of website privacy policies (i.e., in natural language) with annotations that specify data practices in the text. Each privacy policy was read and annotated by three graduate students in law.
  2. Princeton-Leuven Longitudinal Corpus of Privacy Policies - A reference dataset of over 1 million privacy policy snapshots from more than 100,000 websites, spanning over two decades.
