Privacy Xplorer's Data and Models



Privacy Xplorer applies advanced NLP models to a corpus of 1M privacy policies over a 20 year period, providing regulators with the first longitudinal analysis of GDPR adoption that is also 150 times the size of prior work.

Privacy Xplorer leverages fine-tuned LegalBERT and outperforms the prior baseline established by privacy policy parsing tool Polisis by as much as 30%. Read more on our natural language processing approach and model results here.
