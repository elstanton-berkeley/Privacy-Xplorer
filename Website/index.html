Privacy Xplorer

Learn more about our project

Many existing privacy products focus only on the most recent privacy policies. While they help to educate users around how well a company is doing with things like simple scores, they lack a trend analysis. We leveraged the Princeton-Leuven Longitudinal Corpus of Privacy Policies which included over 1 million snapshots from 100,000 websites across two decades.

Our project also helps to bring to light the impact of the General Data Project Regulation (GDPR) which came into effect on May 25, 2018. Leveraging our historical view helps to show which companies were leaders in this space and which were laggers. Say something else here...
